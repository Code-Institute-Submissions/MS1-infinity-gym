Lessons:

Bootcamp

Bootcamp is a (group) lesson, where you can work on your muscles. It is a lesson where you train your whole body and that is accessible to all levels. You can follow this lesson in our gym completely at your own pace. Even if you've been away for a while. Bootcamp is the perfect lesson. You determine your own challenge experiencing any pressure. With fun training at the right level, you can quickly achieve results and stay motivated.

Yoga

Yoga is a class with movements from yoga, pilates and thai chi. The right balance between exercise and relaxation. The exercises make the muscles stronger and more flexible, but still give you a relaxed feeling afterwards. After class you feel calm and relaxed!

Body Step

BodyStep is an energetic workout it will improve your fitness, endurance and coordination. In addition, you also burn a lot of calories! BodyStep is a full-body workout and suitable for all levels. The lessons are easy to follow for beginners, but also a challenge for advanced! During this lesson you perform several exercises in a row with small rest periods. You will notice that your heart rate and breathing will be accelerated, which will trigger your fat burning! BodyStep is therefore a good lesson to lose weight and improve your fat burning!

Pilates

Pilates is the perfect group lesson when you want to lose weight and become stronger and relax at the same time. With this lesson, you can find the ultimate balance between body and mind. The focus during the exercise is concentration and stability. Our pilates lesson is a low impact class, which means that all exercises are performed at a low pace. This allows the lesson for both beginners and advanced students.
